TRAINS NTMs through i-TIP

Non-Tariff Measures for more than 50 countries classified according to the International Classification of NTMs. Each measure has a detailed description, a list of affected products classified with HS codes, and a list of partners affected. The link to the online regulation text is provided when available.

Data can be retreived by selecting one or several countries, measure types, products and partners.

TRAINS Tariffs through WITS

Tariffs (MFN and preferential) for 150 countries at the tariff line level. Depending on the country the data are available from 1988 onwards.

Tariff line data get be retreived from WITS home > Advanced Query >Bulk Download (TRAINS).

Aggregated indicators can be downloaded from WITS Home > Advanced Query > Tariff and Trade Analysis

Aggregated indicators can also be downloaded from databank.worldbank.org http://databank.worldbank.org/data/reports.aspx?source=UNCTAD-~-Trade-Analysis-Information-System-%28TRAINS%29#
